My views on education

By: Dhruv

Education should inspire curiosity, nurture individuality, and foster a love for learning. It's not just about preparing for jobs; it's about shaping well-rounded, empathetic citizens. Quality education should be accessible to all, and teachers play a pivotal role in this journey. Let's embrace technology but not forget the value of human connection. Inclusivity, diversity, and critical thinking should be at the core. The true measure of success is not grades but the positive impact on society. It's time to reimagine education as a path to enlightenment and empowerment, not just employment.
